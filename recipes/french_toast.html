Recipe-French Toast

Description

French toast, also known as pain perdu, is a classic breakfast dish that is made by soaking slices of bread in a mixture of eggs, milk, and cinnamon, and then frying them until they are golden brown. The result is a deliciously sweet and eggy breakfast that is perfect for weekend mornings or brunch.

Ingredients

  • Slices of bread (thick slices work best)
  • Eggs
  • Milk or cream
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Butter or oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, and cinnamon. Add sugar if desired.
  2. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides evenly.
  3. In a non-stick pan, heat a small amount of butter or oil over medium heat.
  4. Place the coated bread slices into the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  5. Repeat the process with the remaining slices of bread, adding more butter or oil as needed.
  6. Serve the French toast hot, topped with butter, syrup, fresh fruit, or any other desired toppings.
